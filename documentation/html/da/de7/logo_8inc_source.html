3 /*
4 The official POV-Ray Logo
5 Designed by Chris Colefax
6 
7 This logo was the one that won the POV-Ray Logo Contest
8 held and organized by Rune S. Johansen in year 2000 and
9 monitored by the POV-Team.
10 
11 Comment from Chris Colefax:
12 
13 "This logo (or symbol, rather) attempts to encapsulate a
14 number of ideas. Firstly, the symbol represents an eye with
15 a ray being traced from it, capturing the fundamentally
16 visual nature of POV-Ray and the rendering process POV-Ray
17 uses. More obviously, it's a P, and both an O and a V can
18 be seen in it as well, making the symbol specific to (and
19 immediately identifiable with) POV-Ray, rather than
20 raytracing in general. The basic symbol itself is a simple
21 CSG construction composed of five POV-Ray primitives. The
22 simplicity of the symbol means that it can be presented
23 recognisably in a range of ways, from a graphical 2D logo
24 to a full blown rendering, or incorporated discreetly into
25 other designs and images."
26 
27 
28 Prism version and bevel version by Rune S. Johansen.
29 
30 */
